The Constantine

"I guess there is a plan for all of us. I had to die twice to figure that out.He work in mysterious ways. Some people like it. Some people don’t," John Constantine


Constantine is born with a curse that he could see demons. According to the story the world is built in three planes, heaven, hell, and the earth where humans live. God maintains the balance of evil and goodness in the earth by allowing the half breed to stay on earth. The half breed are able to manipulate humans and can make them do things that the evil wish to do. Constantine with his witchcraft frees people from the demons and devils and sends devils back to their hell. Most of the current residents in the hell are sent there by Constantine and they are waiting for his arrival especially the evil king Lucifer.

The movie takes through a roller coaster of demons and evils. Though the movie comes in the genre of horror, there weren’t any scary instances. Though it is not recommended to children as they might create a hallucination of seeing objects or things later wards. Overall the movie was thriller but, the devil seems less strong and as usual, the hero wins all the fights. There isn’t an instance where hero is losing. It leads to the typical movie ending where the hero wins all the fights and which is expected by the viewers as they themselves don’t want to see a sad or tragic ending.
